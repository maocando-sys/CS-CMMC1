CMMC LVL 1 CS : 3. Policies

Created by Manuel Ocando, last modified on Feb 03, 2026

Purpose

This section identifies the policies established by the organization to meet CMMC Level 1 requirements for the protection of Federal Contract Information (FCI).

These policies define mandatory requirements for personnel and systems within the defined CMMC Level 1 scope.

Policy Framework

The organization maintains a set of formal, management-approved policies aligned to CMMC Level 1 (FAR 52.204-21)control domains.

Policies are:

  • Documented

  • Approved by management

  • Communicated to applicable personnel

  • Enforced through technical and administrative controls

Applicable CMMC Level 1 Policies

The following policies apply to all in-scope systems and users:

  • Access Control Policy

  • Identification and Authentication Policy

  • Media Protection Policy

  • Physical Protection Policy

  • System and Communications Protection Policy

  • System and Information Integrity Policy

Each policy defines:

  • Purpose and scope

  • Required controls

  • Roles and responsibilities

  • Enforcement expectations

  • Review and maintenance requirements

Policy Applicability

Policies apply only to:

  • In-scope systems

  • In-scope users

  • Approved environments handling FCI

Out-of-scope systems and users are not subject to CMMC Level 1 policy requirements.

Policy Enforcement

Compliance with policies is mandatory.

Violations may result in:

  • Revocation of system access

  • Disciplinary action

  • Contractual consequences where applicable

Policy Review and Updates

All CMMC Level 1 policies are reviewed:

  • At least annually

  • When regulatory or contractual requirements change

  • When system or operational changes affect policy applicability

Approved updates are documented and communicated as required.
