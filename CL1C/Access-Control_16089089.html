CMMC LVL 1 CS : Access Control

Created by Manuel Ocando on Feb 03, 2026

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Access Control Policy is to ensure that access to organizational information systems and Federal Contract Information (FCI) is limited to authorized users, authorized processes, and authorized devices, and that such access is restricted to approved transactions and functions in accordance with CMMC Level 1 requirements (FAR 52.204-21).

2. Scope

This policy applies to:

  • All employees, contractors, and third parties

  • All information systems that store, process, or transmit FCI

  • All methods of access, including local, remote, and cloud-based access

Systems and users identified as in-scope for CMMC Level 1 are governed by this policy without exception.

3. Policy Statements

3.1 Authorized Access

Access to organizational systems is granted only to individuals who:

  • Have been explicitly authorized by management

  • Require access to perform assigned job responsibilities

  • Have been issued a unique user account

Shared, generic, or anonymous accounts are prohibited on systems handling FCI.

3.2 Least Privilege

User access is restricted to the minimum level necessary to perform assigned functions. Permissions are assigned based on defined roles and are not granted by default.

Users are prohibited from performing actions outside their authorized role or system permissions.

3.3 Authorized Transactions and Functions

Users are permitted to execute only those transactions and functions that are explicitly authorized for their role.

System configurations and access permissions are implemented to prevent unauthorized actions, including unauthorized data access, modification, or transmission.

3.4 Control of Federal Contract Information (FCI)

The organization controls the flow of FCI by:

  • Restricting FCI access to authorized users and systems

  • Limiting storage of FCI to approved locations

  • Prohibiting unauthorized transfer of FCI between systems or to external entities

Transmission of FCI is permitted only through approved and protected communication mechanisms.

3.5 External System Access

Access to organizational systems from external systems or devices is:

  • Explicitly authorized

  • Authenticated

  • Restricted to approved access methods and managed devices

Unauthorized external access, including access from unmanaged or unapproved devices, is prohibited.

3.6 Account Management

User accounts are:

  • Provisioned following documented approval

  • Modified when job roles change

  • Disabled or revoked promptly upon termination or loss of authorization

Account reviews are conducted periodically to ensure continued authorization.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

Management

  • Approves user access requests

  • Ensures access aligns with business and contractual requirements

System Owners / Administrators

  • Implement access controls

  • Enforce least privilege

  • Maintain access control configurations

Users

  • Access systems only as authorized

  • Protect authentication credentials

  • Report unauthorized access or anomalies

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate revocation of system access

  • Disciplinary action

  • Contractual or legal consequences where applicable

6. Compliance Mapping

This policy satisfies the following CMMC Level 1 Access Control practices:

  • AC.L1-3.1.1 – Limit system access to authorized users

  • AC.L1-3.1.2 – Limit system access to authorized transactions and functions

  • AC.L1-3.1.20 – Control the flow of Federal Contract Information

  • AC.L1-3.1.22 – Limit system access to authorized users of external systems

7. Review and Maintenance

This policy is reviewed:

  • At least annually

  • Upon significant system, personnel, or contractual changes

  • When required to maintain compliance with CMMC requirements

Approved revisions are documented and communicated to applicable personnel.
