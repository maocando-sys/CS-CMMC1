CMMC LVL 1 CS : System and Communications Protection

Created by Manuel Ocando on Feb 03, 2026

1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements to protect system boundaries and communications involving Federal Contract Information (FCI), in accordance with CMMC Level 1.

2. Scope

Applies to:

  • All in-scope systems

  • All communications transmitting FCI

3. Policy Statements

3.1 Boundary Protection

System boundaries are defined and protected through approved security mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.

3.2 Protection of FCI in Transit

FCI transmitted across networks is protected against unauthorized disclosure using approved transmission safeguards.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • System Administrators implement boundary and communication protections

  • Users comply with approved communication methods

5. Enforcement

Unauthorized transmission methods or bypassing protections may result in access revocation.

6. Compliance Mapping

  • SC.L1-3.13.1 – Monitor and control system communications

  • SC.L1-3.13.5 – Protect FCI in transit

7. Review

Reviewed annually or upon system changes.
