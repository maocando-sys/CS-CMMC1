CMMC LVL 1 CS : Physical Protection

Created by Manuel Ocando, last modified on Feb 03, 2026

1. Purpose

This policy ensures that physical access to systems and facilities containing Federal Contract Information (FCI) is restricted to authorized individuals, in accordance with CMMC Level 1.

2. Scope

Applies to:

  • Facilities housing systems that handle FCI

  • All personnel and visitors

3. Policy Statements

3.1 Physical Access Control

Physical access to systems and areas containing FCI is limited to authorized individuals using approved access control mechanisms.

3.2 Visitor Control

Visitors are:

  • Logged upon entry

  • Escorted when accessing areas containing FCI

  • Prevented from unsupervised access

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Management ensures physical controls are enforced

  • Personnel comply with visitor escort requirements

5. Enforcement

Unauthorized physical access may result in disciplinary action or removal from facilities.

6. Compliance Mapping

  • PE.L1-3.10.1 – Limit physical access to systems

  • PE.L1-3.10.3 – Escort visitors

7. Review

Reviewed annually or upon facility changes.
