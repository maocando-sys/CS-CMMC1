CMMC LVL 1 CS : Media Protection

Created by Manuel Ocando, last modified on Feb 03, 2026

1. Purpose

This policy defines requirements for the sanitization and disposal of media containing Federal Contract Information (FCI) to prevent unauthorized disclosure, in accordance with CMMC Level 1.

2. Scope

Applies to:

  • Digital and physical media containing FCI

  • All personnel handling such media

3. Policy Statements

3.1 Media Identification

Media containing FCI is identified and handled according to its sensitivity.

3.2 Media Sanitization

Media containing FCI is sanitized or destroyed prior to disposal or reuse using approved methods.

3.3 Media Disposal

Disposal is performed in a manner that prevents recovery of FCI, either internally or through approved third-party services.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Management approves disposal methods

  • Personnel follow handling and disposal procedures

5. Enforcement

Failure to properly sanitize or dispose of media may result in disciplinary action.
